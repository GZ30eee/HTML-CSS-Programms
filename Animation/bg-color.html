The background color of this page will change!

  • animation: colorChange 5s infinite; - This is a shorthand property for setting all the individual animation properties at once (name, duration, timing function, delay, iteration count, direction, fill mode, and play state).
  • @keyframes colorChange; - This is where we define what the animation should do at certain times. In this case, it changes the background color of the body from red to green to blue, causing a color change effect.
