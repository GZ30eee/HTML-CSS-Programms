This text will jiggle!

  • animation: jiggle 0.3s infinite; - This is a shorthand property for setting all the individual animation properties at once (name, duration, timing function, delay, iteration count, direction, fill mode, and play state).
  • @keyframes jiggle; - This is where we define what the animation should do at certain times. In this case, it rotates the text back and forth around its center, causing a jiggling effect.
